# 💸 Money Abundance - 30 Days Mind Hack

A completely private, web-based tracker designed to help you play **The Prosperity Game** (a popular manifestation and mindset exercise).

## 🧠 The Concept
The goal of this "mind hack" is to train your brain to get comfortable with having and spending large amounts of wealth, removing subconscious "scarcity mindset" blocks.

* **The Rule:** You have to mentally "spend" the exact amount of your daily allowance. You aren't supposed to just save it or invest it in boring index funds. 
* **The Challenge:** Spending $1,000 on Day 1 is easy. But by Day 15 you have to spend $15,000, and by Day 30, you have to figure out how to spend $30,000 in a single day. 
* **The Goal:** It forces you to stretch your imagination, research high-end items, and break through mental limits about what you are allowed to want or afford.

## 🎮 How to Play
1. **Day 1:** Open the Day 1 tab. You are given an allowance of $1,000. Type in what you are "buying" (e.g., "Spa Day" for $700, "Groceries" for $300) and hit **+ Add**. Your goal is to get the balance badge at the bottom to hit exactly `$0` so it turns green.
2. **Day 2:** Your allowance increases to $2,000. You must think of entirely new things to spend it on!
3. **Days 3 to 30:** The daily limit keeps growing by $1,000 each day. The colored dots on the tabs will help you see which days you have successfully balanced.
4. **Summary Tab:** Check your overall progress here. 

If you complete all 30 days and zero out your balance every single day, you will have successfully simulated spending **$465,000** in one month.

## 🔒 Privacy & Data
This application is 100% serverless. There is no backend database. 
* All of your entries and spending habits are saved directly to your device's local browser storage. 
* No one else can see what you enter, and you cannot see anyone else's entries.
* **Note:** If you clear your browser cache, your data will be reset. Use the **Export** button to download a backup file of your progress, which you can **Import** later if needed.
