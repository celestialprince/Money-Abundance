The Prosperity Game
Money Abundance · 30 Days Mind Hack

A psychology trick to break your scarcity mindset by forcing your brain to get comfortable spending large amounts of money — every single day.

1
Open Day 1. You have an allowance of $1,000 CAD. Type what you'd "buy" and the amount, then hit + Add. Try to get your balance to exactly $0 — the badge turns green when you nail it.
2
Each day the allowance grows by $1,000. Day 2 is $2,000. Day 15 is $15,000. Day 30 is $30,000. Over 30 days you simulate spending $465,000 total.
3
Check your dots. The coloured dot on each tab tells you its status — ● balance remaining, ● perfectly zeroed, ● overspent.
4
Use the Insights tab to search entries across all days, view your daily spending chart, and see a category breakdown of where your imaginary money went.
⚠️ The Real Challenge
You must actually spend it — no savings, no index funds, and you cannot repeat purchases. No buying 15 MacBooks to clear Day 15. You have to research and imagine a real, evolving lifestyle every single day. That's where the mindset shift happens.
